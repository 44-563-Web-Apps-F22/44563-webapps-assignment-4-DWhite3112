Princess IDA

ACT II SCENE Gardens in Castle Adamant. A river runs across the back of the stage, crossed by a rustic bridge. Castle Adamant in the distance. Girl Graduates discovered seated at the feet of Lady Psyche

(Enter Lady Blanche. All stand up demurely) Blanche: Attention, ladies, while I read to you The Princess Ida's list of punishments. The first is Sacharissa. She's expelled! All: Expelled! Blan.: Expelled, because although she knew No man of any kind may pass our walls, She dared to bring a set of chessmen here! Sach.: (Crying) I meant no harm; they're only men of wood! Blan.: They're men with whom you give each other mate, And that's enough! The next is Chloe. Chloe: Ah! Blan.: Chloe will lose three terms, for yesterday, When looking through her drawing-book, I found A sketch of a perambulator!
